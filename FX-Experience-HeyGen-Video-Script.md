# FX Experience - HeyGen Video Script

**Duration:** ~4 minutes | **Language:** English | **Tone:** Executive, confident, warm
**Avatar:** Professional presenter (business casual)
**Target Audience:** US-based decision-maker evaluating the FX Experience platform

---

## SCENE 1 — Opening Hook (0:00 - 0:25)

**[Visual suggestion: FX Experience logo animation → platform dashboard overview]**

> Hi, thank you for taking a few minutes to watch this. I know your time is valuable, so let me get straight to the point.
>
> You asked to see what we built for the Freudenberg Experience program — and I'm excited to walk you through it.
>
> FX Experience is a digital platform designed to transform how Freudenberg manages its global exchange and talent development program. Instead of spreadsheets, emails, and disconnected tools, everything now lives in one place — intelligent, integrated, and ready to scale.

---

## SCENE 2 — The Problem We Solve (0:25 - 0:55)

**[Visual suggestion: split screen showing "before" (scattered emails, spreadsheets) vs "after" (clean dashboard)]**

> Let me start with the challenge. Running a global exchange program across multiple countries involves a lot of moving parts: enrollment workflows, diary tracking, mentor coordination, innovation challenges, learning paths, compliance documentation, and executive reporting.
>
> Before FX Experience, most of this was managed manually — through email threads, shared drives, and disconnected HR tools. That meant lost context, delayed approvals, and no unified view of program impact.
>
> We changed that.

---

## SCENE 3 — Platform Overview (0:55 - 1:40)

**[Visual suggestion: animated walkthrough of the Home dashboard with KPIs, activity feed, and world map]**

> When a participant logs in, they land on a personalized dashboard. Right away, they see their program progress, open exchange opportunities across the globe, active innovation challenges, and recent activity from their peers.
>
> The platform supports four user roles — Participants, Mentors, HR Administrators, and Program Managers — each with a tailored experience. What a participant sees is very different from what an admin sees, and that's by design.
>
> Everything is available in three languages: English, Portuguese, and German — reflecting Freudenberg's global footprint.

---

## SCENE 4 — Key Modules Deep Dive (1:40 - 3:00)

### Exchange Center (1:40 - 2:00)

**[Visual suggestion: Exchange Center page with opportunity cards]**

> The Exchange Center is where participants discover and apply for international assignments. Each opportunity shows the host location, duration, requirements, and application status. The entire approval workflow — from manager sign-off to HR validation — happens digitally, with full audit trail.

### Innovation Labs (2:00 - 2:20)

**[Visual suggestion: Innovation Labs with challenge cards and idea submission]**

> Innovation Labs brings real business challenges from Freudenberg units into the platform. Participants submit ideas, collaborate across regions, and leadership can track innovation pipeline metrics in real time. This isn't theoretical — these are actual problems the business needs solved, and the platform captures the solutions.

### Learning Hub (2:20 - 2:35)

**[Visual suggestion: Learning Hub with course cards and progress indicators]**

> The Learning Hub offers structured courses and development paths. Content can be created by program administrators using our AI-assisted content generation tool. Participants track their progress, complete quizzes, and earn completion certificates — all within the platform.

### AI Integration (2:35 - 3:00)

**[Visual suggestion: Freudy chatbot interaction + A3 report generation]**

> One of the most powerful features is our AI layer. We integrated an AI assistant called Freudy that answers questions about the program, helps participants navigate the platform, and provides contextual guidance based on their role and progress.
>
> On the reporting side, the platform can generate A3 reports semi-automatically by summarizing diary entries, mentor feedback, and key milestones using AI. What used to take hours of manual compilation now takes minutes.

## SCENE 5 — Design & Experience (3:00 - 3:25)

**[Visual suggestion: side-by-side light/dark mode, glassmorphism UI elements, gold accents on tab bar]**

> On the design front, we're implementing a premium visual language we call Glass and Gold. Inspired by modern interface design trends, the platform uses glassmorphism effects for depth and elegance, with gold accent details for active states and premium highlights.
>
> It works beautifully in both light and dark modes, and it's fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile. The goal is simple: when someone opens this platform, it should feel like a world-class product — because that's what Freudenberg deserves.

---

## SCENE 6 — Business Value & Closing (3:25 - 4:00)

**[Visual suggestion: executive dashboard with KPI metrics, ROI indicators]**

> So what does this mean for the business? Three things.
>
> First, operational efficiency. Digital workflows replace manual processes, cutting administrative overhead by an estimated forty percent.
>
> Second, visibility. Leadership gets a real-time dashboard with program KPIs — active participants, completion rates, innovation metrics, and business impact indicators — all in one view.
>
> Third, scalability. This platform is built to grow. Whether Freudenberg runs the program with fifty participants or five hundred, the architecture handles it.
>
> I invite you to explore the platform yourself — navigate it, test the workflows, interact with the AI assistant. I'm confident you'll see the value firsthand.
>
> Thank you for your time, and I look forward to your feedback.
