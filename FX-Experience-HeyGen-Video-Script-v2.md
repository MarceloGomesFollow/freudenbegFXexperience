# FX Experience — HeyGen Video Script v2

## Overview

A comprehensive presentation of the FX Experience platform for Freudenberg Group, using a sophisticated **Cyanotype Blueprint** visual style to explain its features and business value.

**Duration:** ~4 minutes (239 seconds)
**Language:** English
**Format:** A-roll (narrator on screen) with Cyanotype overlays

---

## Visual Style: Cyanotype Blueprint

**Theme:** 1890 Engineering Drawing Archive

| Token | Color | Usage |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Blueprint Blue | `#1A3A6B` | Background |
| Cyanotype White | `#E8F4F8` | Linework |
| Blueprint Fade | `#2E5F9E` | Secondary detail |
| Linen Cream | `#F0E8D0` | Border edges |

**Rules:** All linework hairline-weight, no fills — pure outline orthographic drafting. Room labels in stencil lettering. Captions in white blueprint stencil lettering. Narrator appears in all A-roll scenes.
